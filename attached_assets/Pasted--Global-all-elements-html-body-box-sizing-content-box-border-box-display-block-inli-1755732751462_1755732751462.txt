📑 Global (all elements: *, html, body)

box-sizing: content-box | border-box

display: block | inline | inline-block | flex | grid | inline-flex | inline-grid | contents | none

visibility: visible | hidden | collapse

opacity: 0–1

overflow: visible | hidden | scroll | auto | clip

position: static | relative | absolute | fixed | sticky

top/right/bottom/left: <length> | <percentage> | auto

z-index: <integer> | auto

📜 Text & Inline (p, span, strong, em, a, small, etc.)

font-family: system fonts, custom stacks

font-size: <length> | <percentage> | xx-small → xx-large

font-weight: 100–900 | normal | bold | lighter | bolder

font-style: normal | italic | oblique

text-decoration: none | underline | overline | line-through

text-transform: none | uppercase | lowercase | capitalize

letter-spacing: <length>

word-spacing: <length>

line-height: <number> | <length> | normal

color: <color>

white-space: normal | nowrap | pre | pre-line | pre-wrap

text-align: left | right | center | justify | start | end

text-shadow: <offset-x> <offset-y> <blur> <color>

📦 Block Containers (div, section, article, aside, header, footer, main)

margin: <length> | auto

padding: <length>

border: <width> <style> <color>

border-radius: <length> | <percentage>

background-color: <color>

background-image: url(...) | linear-gradient(...) | none

background-size: auto | cover | contain | <length> | <percentage>

background-position: left | center | right | top | bottom | <length> | <percentage>

box-shadow: <offset-x> <offset-y> <blur> <spread> <color>

📊 Lists (ul, ol, li)

list-style-type: disc | circle | square | decimal | decimal-leading-zero | none

list-style-position: inside | outside

list-style-image: url(...) | none

🔗 Links (a)

Inherits inline text properties

cursor: pointer | default | not-allowed | help | wait

text-decoration: (commonly underline / none)

:hover, :active, :visited, :focus: pseudo-class styles

🖼️ Media (img, video, canvas, svg)

width: <length> | <percentage> | auto

height: <length> | <percentage> | auto

max-width: <length> | <percentage> | none

max-height: <length> | <percentage> | none

object-fit: fill | contain | cover | none | scale-down

object-position: <position> | <percentage>

📝 Forms (input, textarea, select, button, label)

appearance: auto | none

width, height: <length> | auto

padding, margin: <length>

border: <width> <style> <color>

border-radius: <length>

background: color | gradient | image

color: <color>

cursor: text | pointer | default | not-allowed

outline: <width> <style> <color> | none

resize (for textarea): none | both | horizontal | vertical

🎛️ Layout Systems
Flex (display: flex | inline-flex)

flex-direction: row | row-reverse | column | column-reverse

flex-wrap: nowrap | wrap | wrap-reverse

justify-content: flex-start | flex-end | center | space-between | space-around | space-evenly

align-items: flex-start | flex-end | center | stretch | baseline

align-content: same as align-items (when wrapping)

Child: flex: none | <grow> <shrink> <basis>

Child: align-self: auto | flex-start | flex-end | center | baseline | stretch

Grid (display: grid | inline-grid)

grid-template-columns: <track-list> | none

grid-template-rows: <track-list> | none

grid-gap: <length>

justify-items: start | end | center | stretch

align-items: start | end | center | stretch

Child: grid-column: <start> / <end>

Child: grid-row: <start> / <end>

🪄 Animations & Transitions

transition: <property> <duration> <timing-function> <delay>

animation: <name> <duration> <timing-function> <delay> <iteration-count> <direction> <fill-mode> <play-state>

transform: translate(...) | scale(...) | rotate(...) | skew(...) | matrix(...)

transform-origin: <x> <y>
